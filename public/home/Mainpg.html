About Us

Founded in 1917, the Ramjas College library has been an integral part of the institution's quest for academic excellence. Guided by a vision to foster a thriving environment of knowledge and learning, the library stands as a cornerstone of intellectual growth, serving as a beacon for students and faculty alike.

Over the years, the library has evolved into one of Delhi University's largest and most esteemed libraries. It boasts a diverse collection of approximately 130,000 printed volumes, including books, textbooks, and bound volumes of journals. The library's resources cater to the diverse academic needs of students and faculty across various disciplines.

With a rich history of empowering generations through access to diverse resources, the library plays a pivotal role in shaping visionary leaders of tomorrow. It is not just a repository of books but a center of academic engagement, promoting a culture of learning and research within the college. As Ramjas College continues to thrive as a prestigious institution of higher learning, the library remains a symbol of knowledge and a hub of intellectual exploration for all who pass through its doors.

Our services

Opac

The college library offers a user-friendly OPAC (Online Public Access Catalog) service, enabling students and faculty to conveniently search and access a vast collection of books .

E-Resources

The college library provides access to a wide range of electronic resources, offering students and faculty a wealth of scholarly articles, e-books, databases, and online journals.

Reference Service

The college provides a comprehensive reference service to support the reference service plays a crucial role in providing timely and accurate information, empowering users.

Current Awareness

The library offers a Current Awareness Service (CAS) to keep users informed about the latest developments in their fields of interest.

Book Lending service

The library provides a book lending service, allowing users to borrow books for a specific period,depends on the type of user.

Instant messaging

The library offers an instant messaging service for quick and convenient communication with library staff for assistance and queries.
