Acquisition Section

This section plays a crucial role in acquiring books for the library. It is responsible for collecting approvals or recommendations from faculty members, which are then reviewed by members of the Library Committee from various departments. Once approved, the library interacts with empanelled vendors of the University of Delhi to place orders for the books. After receiving the books, they are accessioned in this section and later transferred to the Technical Processing Section for cataloguing, classification, indexing, and database entry.

Technical Processing Section

This section plays a vital role in processing the procured books, acting as a bridge between the Acquisition and Circulation Sections. It is responsible for classifying and cataloging the books that have been acquired. Additionally, the section selects book covers for display in the New Arrivals Display Area for a period of two weeks. After this duration, the old covers are replaced with new ones, and this process continues each year. The processed books are then stored in stacks for circulation. Furthermore, a core list of the processed books for each subject is prepared and sent to the respective Library Committee Member at the end of the year.

Periodical Section

This section plays a key role in handling the subscription and renewal of Print Journals, Magazines, and Newspapers. It takes responsibility for managing the subscriptions and ensuring timely renewals. In cases where issues are not received as expected, this section follows up with the publisher or vendor, sending reminders and taking appropriate actions for non-received issues. At the end of the year, a core list of loose issues of periodicals is compiled, and these issues are sent for binding to preserve them and maintain the library's collection.

Membership Section

This section is responsible for handling the issuance of Membership Cards, which serve as College ID cards, to both students and faculty members. Additionally, the section is in charge of renewing and canceling cards/memberships for college users. They ensure that all eligible students and faculty receive their ID cards promptly and that any necessary updates or cancellations are processed efficiently. This helps maintain an accurate and up-to-date record of college members and facilitates their access to various college facilities and services.

Circulation Section

This section plays a central role in the Library's circulation process, which involves the issuance and return of documents to library users. Additionally, it handles the reservation of books for users who wish to borrow specific materials. Serving as a vital link between users and books, this section ensures a smooth and efficient borrowing experience for library patrons. It assists users in accessing the books they need and facilitates the timely return of borrowed items, promoting seamless interactions between the library's resources and its users.

Reference Section

This section helps library users find the information they need from various sources and manages the reference collection. It serves as a bridge between users and the vast information resources available in the library, providing expert guidance and research assistance.

Property Counter

This section, located outside the library, serves as a designated counter for users to keep their bags and belongings.
