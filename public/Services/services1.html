CIRCULATION SERVICE

Circulation Services assists library patrons with the location and circulation of library materials. Functions include check-outs (Issue )and check-ins (Return) of materials, collection of fines and other charges, re-shelving of library materials, and maintaining order in shelving areas. Circulation Services also assists patrons with general questions about the College Libraries. The Circulation counter open 9:30 AM to 4:30 PM.

MEMBERSHIP SERVICE

Library membership is free to all students, faculty and staff of the college. The college library registered each member of college and issued a membership card for each students, teaching and non-teaching staff

READING ROOM SERVICE

College library provides a reading room facility to students. There are two reading rooms available on both floors. A special provision has been made for the students to read books in the cool and conducive atmosphere of the library.

CURRENT AWARENESS SERVICE

College library provides the current awareness service to the users through new arrivals display or display the list of new books. The purpose of CAS is to inform the users about new acquisitions in their libraries

Other Books

This section holds a treasure trove of literary gems, consisting of rare books and valuable antiquities that bear witness to the passage of time. As you ascend to the first and second floors of the book stack area, you will be transported to a realm of history, culture, and intellectual heritage.

REFERENCE SERVICE

Service provided by libraries whereby patrons are assisted in the location and retrieval of information relevant to their information needs. It is the personal interaction between the users and the staff. There are various reference materials available i.e. encyclopedias, dictionaries, biographies, bibliographies etc.

OPAC(Online Public Access Catalogue)

The OPAC can be used to search the holdings of the library and to request or reserve books, to search items available in the library, to find a book on the shelf. Users may search the OPAC for a book, note the call number, and search the relevant shelves. Users can ask for help from library staff in case of difficulty in finding the required book.

SYLLABUS AND LAST YEAR QUESTION PAPERS

Syllabus and last year's question papers are provided by the library to users on demand.

ENABLING UNIT SERVICE

College library also provides services to physically challenged and visually impaired students. Separate sitting room facility available within the library and also one special enabling unit are established for satisfying their information needs, and for solving their day to day issues..

Property Counter

The Library offers the facility of property counter to its users. Users are required to deposit their Bags (excluding valuable items such as money, passport, credit/debit cards/mobile phones etc.) with the property counter attendant before entering into the library. But they are advised not to carry any valuable belongings / cash / jewellery etc. for which they themselves be responsible.
