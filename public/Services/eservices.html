Electronic Resources

  • The library subscribes electronic resources provided by National Library and Information Services Infrastructure for Scholarly Content (N-LIST), which is jointly executed by the e-ShodhSindhu Consortium,INFLIBNET Centre and the INDEST-AICTE Consortium, IIT Delhi.

  • It provides cross-subscription to e-resources subscribed by the two Consortia, i.e. subscription to INDEST-AICTE resources for universities and also e-ShodhSindhu resources for technical institutions.

  • It provides access to e-resources to students and faculty from colleges.

  • The authorized users from colleges can remotely access e-resources and download articles required by them directly from the publisher's website once they are duly authenticated as authorized users through servers deployed at the INFLIBNET Centre.

  • The number of electronic resources subscribed under N-LIST Programme are as follows:

    1. E-journals (Full text): 6293
    2. E-books: 1,60,809

  • The library gets access to all the electronic resources subscribed by Delhi University Library System.
    Website: https://nlist.inflibnet.ac.in/veresources.php
